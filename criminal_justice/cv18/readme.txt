Criminal Victimization, 2018       NCJ 253043

from Criminal Victimization, 2018,  NCJ 253043.  The full report including text		
and graphics in pdf format is available from: https://www.bjs.gov/index.cfm?ty=pbdetail&iid=6686		
		
This report is one in a series.  More recent editions		
may be available.  To view a list of all in the series go to		
https://www.bjs.gov/index.cfm?ty=pbse&sid=6
